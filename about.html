Welcome!

Research in the Hwang Lab focuses on the cognitive and neural dynamics of cognitive control. Learn more on our research and publications pages. We are affiliated with the Department of Psychological and Brain Sciences and the Cognitive Control Collaborative, a super‑lab initiative for researchers studying cognitive control.

We are located at 355W PBSB, 340 Iowa Ave, Iowa City, IA 52242.

Our research is supported by the National Institute of Mental Health.
