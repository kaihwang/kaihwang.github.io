Postdoctoral Position

We are seeking a postdoctoral fellow to join our research team investigating the neural mechanisms of cognitive control and the human thalamocortical system. Research topics will be tailored to the fellow’s skills and interests, with opportunities to lead projects and collaborate across disciplines.

Qualifications:

  • PhD in Psychology, Neuroscience, or a related discipline.
  • Strong interest in cognitive control, brain connectivity, and computational research.
  • Background in human neuroscience and/or cognitive psychology.
  • Experience with neuroimaging (fMRI, EEG/MEG).
  • Strong programming skills (Matlab or Python).

Start Date: Flexible, open until filled.

To Apply: Send a cover letter and CV to kai-hwang@uiowa.edu

Postbaccalaureate Researcher - Cognitive Neuroscience (Note: no longer accepting appliactions)

The Hwang Lab for Neurocognitive Dynamics at the University of Iowa is hiring a paid, full-time post-baccalaureate research assistant / lab manager to support our human cognitive neuroscience projects. Our lab focuses on brain network mechanisms, cognitive control, and neuromodulation, with an emphasis on the human thalamocortical system and executive functions. We use a multimodal approach that includes EEG, brain stimulation, MRI, and computational modeling. More information about the lab can be found on our website: https://kaihwang.github.io/.

The research assistant will be responsible for:

  • Conducting cognitive neuroscience research under the supervision of the lab director and senior members.
  • Performing EEG and behavioral experiments, conducting TUS and MRI experiments, and collecting data.
  • Assisting with neuroimaging data analysis.
  • Recruiting and scheduling research participants.
  • Managing lab tasks and contributing to other research-related activities.

This position is ideal for someone aiming to pursue a research career. For more details, see the University of Iowa post-baccalaureate research internship page . Training and mentorship will be provided.

Required Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Psychology, Neuroscience, Cognitive Science, or a related field.
  • At least two years of research experience.
  • Excellent interpersonal skills, strong organizational skills, and careful attention to detail.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Previous research experience in neuroscience, cognitive psychology, developmental psychology, or cognitive science.
  • Experience with scripting languages such as Python or Matlab.

To apply: Please email your CV and cover letter to kai-hwang@uiowa.edu.
